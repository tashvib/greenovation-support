Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 15, 2025

Introduction

GreenOvation ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our waste classification app.

Information We Collect

1. Camera Data

We require camera access to identify waste items. Photos are processed on your device and are not stored or transmitted to our servers.

2. Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data such as:

  • Learning progress and scores
  • Waste classification attempts
  • App feature usage statistics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide waste classification services
  • Track your learning progress
  • Improve our classification accuracy
  • Enhance app features and user experience

Data Storage and Security

We use Firebase for data storage and authentication. All data is stored securely and transmitted using encryption. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection
  • Request a copy of your data
