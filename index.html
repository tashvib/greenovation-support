Getting Started

GreenOvation helps you learn proper waste disposal through AI-powered recognition. Here's how to use the app:

  • Open the app and allow camera access
  • Point your camera at any waste item
  • Get instant classification and educational information
  • Learn about proper disposal methods

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why does the app need camera access?

The app uses your camera to identify waste items and provide proper disposal guidance.

Q: How accurate is the waste identification?

The app uses advanced AI technology for identification. For best results, ensure good lighting and clear view of the item.

Q: What types of waste can be identified?

The app can identify and categorize items into:

  • Recyclables (paper, plastic, metal)
  • Organic Waste (food scraps, natural materials)
  • E-Waste (electronics, batteries)
  • Hazardous Waste (chemicals, dangerous materials)
